Data-backed recommendations
01 · CITY TRIAGE
Fix Haridwar, Jaipur & Amritsar first
These 3 cities alone drive 41.37% of losses. A focused ops review (delivery cost, staffing, partner rating) here moves the needle faster than a national rollout.
02 · DISTANCE-BASED PRICING
Add a distance surcharge beyond 8km
Profit decays ~12.5% past the 8km mark. A modest distance fee protects margin without discouraging near-radius orders.
03 · CATEGORY-LEVEL DISCOUNTING
Cap discounts on Beverages, Household & Groceries
These categories already carry the thinnest margins and the highest loss share — blanket discounts here disproportionately hurt profitability.
04 · MINIMUM BASKET VALUE
Enforce a floor on low-value orders
Low order value is a top loss driver once delivery cost is netted out — a minimum basket threshold or delivery fee below it protects unit economics.
